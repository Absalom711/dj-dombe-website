About DJ Dombe

I’m DJ Dombe, an emerging artist based in Windhoek, Namibia, blending the worlds of electronic and hip-hop music. Through my project Lazy Eyez, I bring a mix of fresh energy, raw emotion, and authentic vibes — connecting with audiences both locally and across the globe. My journey is driven by rhythm, passion, and a love for sound that tells stories.
